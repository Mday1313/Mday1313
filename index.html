About Me

Web Developer and Freelance Photographer with a background in Anthropology and Peace Studies, with a passion for traveling to new places and experiencing new cultures. I have spent months volunteering in an engine room aboard a floating hospital as well as in a small village in West Africa. I enjoy challenging myself by pushing on the border of my ever expanding comfort zone. Once I start a project, I always see it through to the end. Problem solving is a great strength for me. If I come across something I don’t know how to do, I research it until I do. I work well collaborating with others and working individually. Deadlines are my greatest motivator and I work well under pressure while still maintaining my calm exterior. My experiences have opened my mind and broadened my creativity. I hold a Full-Stack Certification from the University of Central Florida and am experienced in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Jquery, Photoshop and Lightroom. I enjoy designing apps that excite others to want to explore the world as well.
